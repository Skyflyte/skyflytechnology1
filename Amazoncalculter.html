Amazon Price Calculator

If you wish to take your business to the next level and serve thousands of customers without much effort, your best bet is to join Amazon India marketplace. Do so and you start getting orders from all over India. Before you start counting profits you must factor in the charges you pay to Amazon for its services. These include Amazon referral fee, fixed closing fee and shipping fee in addition to taxes. You have to price products accordingly in order to make a profit. Services.amazon.in makes it easy with its online pricing calculator.

Referral Fee

The online Amazon Price Calculator at services.amazon.in is just the tool you need to know just how much profit you will make. The referral fee varies according to product categories. Even within product categories, the Amazon seller fees vary according to product. For instance, referral fee in Electronics, PC and wireless category is 16%. It is 17% for automotive accessories, 5% for laptops and 5% for mobile phones and tablets. If you sell a mobile phone at Rs 10000 you pay Rs 500 as referral fee and Rs 40 as closing fee. Closing fee Amazon charges a fixed closing fee according to product category. Then you have to add courier charges. Look at the table to know closing fees in general
