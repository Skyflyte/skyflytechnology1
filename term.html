What are Terms and Conditions Agreements? ?

A Terms and Conditions agreement acts as a legal contract between you (the company)
and the user. It's where you maintain your rights to exclude users from your app in the event that
they abuse your website/app, set out the rules for using your service and note other important details and disclaimers.

Having a Terms and Conditions agreement is completely optional.
No laws require you to have one. Not even the super-strict
and wide-reaching General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
